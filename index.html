ABOUT Bridges Alliance

Supporting people with a disability and their families and carers to enjoy a life of connection and fulfilment.

Bridges Alliance’s management and staff are focused on ensuring that the personal journey of our clients, their family, and carers is the best it can be – we listen, we support, we act, and we achieve results as we share the privilege of walking together with you.

OUR FEATURED SERVICES
Our focus is walking the extra mile alongside our clients to achieve their goals and dreams
We offer a personalised practical service which supports our clients to choose and create truly meaningful and fulfilling lives, by listening to their voice. Implementing a strength-based approach and person-centred practice is at the core of our principles. We embrace diversity and provide tailored services to persons from all backgrounds and cultures.

Live the best life with Bridges Alliance!

By building a genuine understanding of our client’s needs and placing this at the heart of our service delivery, we believe we are able to deliver effective lifelong outcomes.

Bridges Alliance is a registered service provider in NSW with the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). We are also a registered Voluntary Out-of-Home Care (VOOHC) provider for children and young people under 18 years.
For quality accredited NDIS service providers who truly understand and value their clients in the Greater Western Sydney and Illawarra regions, choose Bridges Alliance.
